Survey on Construction Noise in Singapore

Dear participant,

We are interested to hear about in your opinions on construction noise in Singapore. Your response will help us understand how much you value a reduction in construction noise in Singapore.

You were chosen at random – you don’t need to be an expert on construction noise to do this survey.

The survey takes only about 30 minutes to complete. Your participation in this survey is entirely voluntary and your responses and identity will be kepy strictly confidential. As part of the survey, we will be playing recorded construction noise to you. If you feel any discomfort when hearing these sounds, you can terminate the survey at any point.

If you have any enquiries, please contact Leong Chi Hoong (chihoong.leong@anu.edu.au; +65 9030 1763).

Section 3: Hearing Acuity Test

In the following section of the questionnaire, we will measure the quietest sound you can hear. For each sound, there are two tests:

  • Descending loudness. We will decrease the loudness of the sound from a clearly audible level. As soon as you stop hearing a sound, click on the box.
  • Ascending loudness. We will increase the volume of the sound from an inaudible level. As soon as you hear a sound, no matter how faint the sound, click on the box.

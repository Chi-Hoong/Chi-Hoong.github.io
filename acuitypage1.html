Section 2: Hearing Acuity Test

In the following section of the questionnaire, we will measure the quietest sound you can hear. As soon as you hear a sound, click on the box. Whatever the sound, and no matter how faint the sound, click on the box as soon as you think you hear it.

Gentle reminder

We will be playing several sounds to you during this test. If you feel any discomfort, please terminate the survey by removing the headphones and informing the surveyor.

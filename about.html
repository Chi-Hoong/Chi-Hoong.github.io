About this Survey

This survey is about construction noise in Singapore and consists of four parts:

  • Part 1: About you. We ask a few questions about you.
  • Part 2: Hearing acuity test. We will play several sounds to understand your sensitivity to sounds.
  • Part 3: Your choices. We ask you to choose between different types of construction noise.
  • Part 4: About you. We ask a few more questions about you.

What can be done?

Construction noise could be reduced if some of the following measures were adopted:

  • Change the equipment used for construction, so that quieter construction equipment is used.
  • Erect noise barriers between the construction site and residences near the site.
  • Plan construction activities so that noisier construction work is carried out when residents are away from home.
  • Have larger buffer distances between construction sites and residences so that the noise can dissipate before it reaches residences.

How much noise levels can be reduced depend on how many of these measures are adopted and how they are combined.

Your Choices

We want to find out how much you are willing to pay for a reduction in construction noise. You will be presented with 8 scenarios, each of which comprises of:

  • A background noise. This is the current level of noise and will be the noise level that you experience if no noise reduction measures are taken.
  • Two options with different noises and an increase in service and conservancy charges (S&CC). Mouse-over the alternatives to hear the different types of noise.

In the first four scenarios, the construction noise occurs in the day, while in the next four scenarios, the construction noise occurs in the night.

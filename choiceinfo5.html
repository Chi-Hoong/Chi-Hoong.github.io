What can be done to reduce construction noise?

To reduce the impact of construction noise, the government could:

  1. Regulate construction equipment and practices in order to encourage construction companies to emit lower levels of noise.
  2. Block the noise emitted from construction sites with noise barriers.
  3. Retrofit windows with sound-proof double glazed windows.
  4. Disallow or restrict construction activities at nights and on weekends as well as public holidays.

Section 2: Valuing a Reduction in Construction Noise

In this section of the survey, we will like to find out how much you are willing to pay for a reduction in construction noise. You will be presented with 8 scenarios. In each scenario, there will be a background noise, which is the current level of noise. When you mouse-over the alternatives, you will hear a different noise as well as an increase in the service and conservancy charges (S&CC). In the first four scenarios, the construction noise occurs in the day, while in the next four scenarios, the construction noise occurs in the night.

Please choose the option you prefer most, taking into account the changes in noise level as well as the change in S&CC. When answering each scenario, only use the information provided in the that scenario, without referencing previous scenarios.

When answering the questions, please consider the following:

  • The option that receives the greatest amount of support will be implemented and all residents will make the payment required for that option;
  • Each construction site is expected to last for a period of 3-years (36 months), so you will have to pay the monthly charges associated with the option you choose for this period; and
  • Your income is limited and you have other expenses.

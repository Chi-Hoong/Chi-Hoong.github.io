Construction Noise in Singapore

In the remainder of this section, you will be presented with 8 scenarios, each of which comprises of:

  • A background noise. This is the current level of noise and will be the noise level that you experience if no noise reduction measures are taken.
  • Two options with different noises and a monthly fee that you will have to pay in order to enjoy the reduction in noise associated with that option. Mouse-over the alternatives to hear the different types of noise.

In the first four scenarios, the construction noise occurs in the day (between 7am and 5pm), while in the next four scenarios, the construction noise occurs in the night (between 5pm and 7am).

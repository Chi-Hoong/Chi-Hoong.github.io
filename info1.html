How will the results of the survey be used?

The results of the survey will inform policies related to the control of construction noise. The project is funded by the Ministry of the Environment and Water Resources, Singapore, and is undertaken with the assistance of the Crawford School of Public Policy from the Australian National University.

While the information gained from this survey may be revealed in research publications and reports, all participants will remain anonymous, unless they explicitly indicate that they are willing to be identified when quoted. Any information provided in answering the questionnaire that might identify anyone as individuals will be held in complete confidence. All information collected during this research will be stored electronically (no names attached) for at least 5 years after the publication of results.

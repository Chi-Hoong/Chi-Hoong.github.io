Construction Noise in Singapore

In the remainder of this section, you will be presented with 8 scenarios. Each of these scenarios comprises of:

  • A background noise. This is the level of noise which you will experience at your house should no noise reduction measures be taken.
  • Two options associated with two different noise reduction measure. Each noise reduction measure leads to a different noise level at your house. Different noise reduction measures also have different costs. Mouse-over the alternatives to hear the different types of noise.

When making your choices, please take the following into account:

  1. Your income is limited and you have other expenses.
  2. The payment will only be collected once for the duration of the construction activity at a site.
  3. The policy which receives the greatest support will be implemented. This means that everyone would have to make the payment associated with that policy.
  4. Your answers are important. The Ministry of the Environment and Water Resources would take your responses into consideration when reviewing policies to regulate construction noise.
